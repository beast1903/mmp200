The Bye Bye Summer Music Festival is a multi-day music festival held on Randall's Island in New York City. The festival features pop and R&B music. Aside from the wide variety of music, the festival features a multitude of popular New York restaurants and food trucks, as well as activities and games. People can enjoy different type of activities and games till the festival starts. It is our goal to make you enjoy every second in the festival.You can also get your merch and represent the most enjoyable festival wherever you go.
